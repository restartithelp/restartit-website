About RestartIT

A local, friendly face for all things tech — bringing patient, professional help right to your door.

Who we are

Tech help you can actually trust

RestartIT was built on a simple idea: technology should make life easier, and getting help with it shouldn't be stressful, confusing or overpriced. We're a local, mobile IT service that comes to you — no shop to drive to, no equipment to unplug and haul across town.

With years of hands-on experience across computers, phones, Wi-Fi and smart-home gear, we've seen just about every tech headache there is — and we fix them in plain language, at a pace that works for you. Whether it's a sluggish laptop, a tricky new device or a Wi-Fi signal that won't reach the back bedroom, we'll sort it out properly.

Most of all, we believe in being honest. Flat-rate pricing means you know the cost before we start, and we'll never upsell you on gear or services you don't need. Just friendly, dependable help from someone in your own community.
